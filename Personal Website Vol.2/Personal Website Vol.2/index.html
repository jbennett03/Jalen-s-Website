    About Me






    Coming from Chicago's South Side, for a large part of my life, I was used to being in areas where everyone looked like me. It wasn't until High School where I really got to learn from and work with people from different backgrounds. It was a wonderful 4 years learning and working with different types of people on challenging projects that have opened my mind to so many new ideas and perspectives, and I am forever grateful to recount these experiences. It's one of the reasons that made me want to pursue engineering at UIUC since I am not only challenged by my course work but I also get to continue experiencing growth through overcoming my own personal challenges throughout engineering and collaborating with my peers.
